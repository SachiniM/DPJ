Welcome To DPJ Constructions

DPJ Construction (Pvt) Ltd. is a prominent leading construction company in Sri Lanka, serving the economic infrastructure sector of the country and is a leading and innovative construction company. The company meets the basic needs of its clients through the expertise it has accumulated over the years.

OUR VISION

To be recognized as a competent, professional and reputed Building and Civil Engineering Construction Company among Employers in local and international context.

OUR MISSION

The company aims to provide high-quality services, meet client requirements, and achieve success through a professional approach, skilled employees, dedication to customer service, strategic partnerships, continuous system improvements, and a focus on environmental management and safety. They also provide necessary support to employees and foster a teamwork concept.
