ABOUT US

Established in 2005, DPJ Constructions has grown to become a leading construction company in Sri Lanka, making an indelible imprint in infrastructure development.

DPJ Constructions was incorporated with a vision to develop the infrastructural facilities of Sri Lanka to become a modern country. We are engaged in diverse sectors of civil engineering including buildings, luxury hotels, recreational facilities, as well as electricity supply networks.

As a premier graded civil engineering company by the Divisional Secretariat at Matara, we are committed to offering superior quality and international standards in our professional services to ensure utmost satisfaction of our valued clientele.

Our team comprises qualified and experienced professionals including Chartered Engineers, Structural Engineers, Quantity Surveyors, Management Professionals and a strong work force of over 175 supporting technical and administrative staff.
